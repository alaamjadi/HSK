Listening and Orientation Center

Organizer: Anna Andreoni

What it is and how it works

The Center for Listening and Guidance for Fragile Personsin the socio-health network in the southern area of ​​Milan is a “window” open to the public (Fragile citizens with their family members) from Monday to Friday (9:30-12:30) The Center is supervised by some Operators coordinated by a Case Manager Nurse (Cinzia Pellegrini), with the support of Consultants who lend their activities free of charge in support of frailties.

First level of listening

as well as recording, in a special form, access (with registration of consent to the use of personal and sensitive data) provides indications for less complex cases. The more complex cases are directed to a second level.

Second listening level,

expert internal consultants with different skills are available, with the possibility of booking in-depth interviews in dedicated slots [eg: interviews with a psychologist, with an expert on legal / legal issues (eg support administrator; residence permits) , reunification) or socio-economic needs. 3rd level consultancy outside the farmhouse (e.g. AssociazioneIncerchio, Casa della Carità, Caritas ambrosiana, WeMi Center).

If there is an appropriate indication, it is proposed to some fragile Citizens who live at home in a condition of relative solitude, free participation in our Socializing Laboratories for Fragile Citizens (also thanks to a collaboration with the Coordination Office of the Social Keepers).

If even the expert internal consultants of our second level do not have the competence to give adequate answers to complex needs, the fragile Citizens who access our Listening Center are directed to the public and third sector social and health services with which MilCare foundation Onlus has established collaborative relationships, thanks to the initiatives of the Territorial Hospital Program for continuity of care in the socio-health network in the southern area of ​​Milan and to the activities promoted by the territorial social table of the Municipality 5 in which the MilCare Onlus Foundation participates.

If you believe that you or a family member need help, you can contact the completely free MilCare listening and orientation center.

How to access the Center

The Center (open to the public from Monday to Saturday, 9:30-12:30) can also be contacted by phone (02 12345678 - toll-free number 800123456 outside opening hours, you can leave a message on the answering machine (you will be called back as soon as possible). It is also possible to send an E-mail to info@MilCare.org : you will receive an answer from the Case Manager Nurse of the Center Cinzia Pellegrini 331 1234567 ).

How to reach us

Access by car: driveway entrance in front of the IEO as indicated on the road map (it is possible to park in the courtyard). From the city center to the driveway access in via Ripamonti 428 (site before the roundabout to enter the IEO); from Tangenziale Ovest, exit Val Tidone-Vigentina: continue towards Milano Centro until the roundabout for IEO with direction reversal towards the entrance of the farmhouse.

Access by public transport: tram 24 (from Piazza Duomo-Piazza Missori to the terminus): from the terminus, bus 99 or 222 to the first stop right at the pedestrian access to MilCare or a short 10-minute walk.

Access by Shuttle: free shuttle service every 30 minutes starting at 09:00 till 18:00 thanks to the IEO Foundation from Piazzale Abbiategrasso (green line underground stop) to the IEO.
