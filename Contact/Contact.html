From the city center to the driveway access in via Ripamonti 428 (site before the roundabout to enter the IEO); from the Tangenziale Ovest, exit Val Tidone-Vigentina: continue towards Milano Centro up to the roundabout for IEO with direction reversal towards the entrance of the farmhouse. Free parking inside Cascina Brandezzata which can be accessed from the driveway entrance as shown in the photo below.

From the center (Piazza Duomo - Via Dogana): Tram n. 24 towards Vigentino to the terminus, then bus no. 99 direction Noverasco or bus n. 222 (extra urban line) for Pieve Emanuele. From Central Station : underground Yellow Line MM3 to the "Crocetta" stop, Tram n. 24 towards Vigentino to the terminus, then bus no. 99 direction Noverasco or bus n. 222 (extra urban line) for Pieve Emanuele.

From the "Abbiategrasso" stop (MM2 Green Line) to the IEO in Via Ripamonti and vice versa. Shuttle terminal Piazza Abbiategrasso, in via dei Missaglia, underground exit. Terminus, in front of the IEO. Duration about 15/20 minutes. Service active from Monday to Friday excluding holidays. The Service is supported by the IEO - CCM Foundation.
